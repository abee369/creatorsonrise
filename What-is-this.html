Why keep track?

Sometimes I see some cool creators that are highlighted and forget to subscribe or remember something that they did and want to go back to check. This happened multiple times and didn't find any resources that kept track of all the creators that were featured. So I decided to start one.

What are the columns?

Date ➡️ The day that the creator/artist is featured

Channel ➡️ It's the creator/artist name and channel link

Type ➡️ Shows wheather they are creator or artist.

Location ➡️ Shows which trending page they are featured on.

Subs Before ➡️ Amount of subs that the creator/artist had the day before they were featured.

Subs After ➡️ Amount of subs that the creator/artist had the day after they were featured.

Subs Today ➡️ Amount of subs that the creator/artist currently have

Uploads Before ➡️ Amount of uploads that the creator/artist had the day before they were featured

Uploads After ➡️ Amount of uploads that the creator/artist had the day before they were featured

Uploads Today ➡️ Amount of uploads that the creator/artist currently have
